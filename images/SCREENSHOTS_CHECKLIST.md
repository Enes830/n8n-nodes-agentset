## Tips for Taking Screenshots

1. **Resolution**: Use a consistent resolution (recommended: 1200px width minimum)
2. **Format**: PNG format preferred for crisp text
3. **Clean UI**: Hide any personal/sensitive information
4. **Focus**: Crop to show only the relevant parts
5. **Annotations**: Consider adding arrows or highlights for important elements

## Recommended Tools

- macOS: Built-in screenshot (Cmd+Shift+4) or CleanShot X
- Browser extension: Full Page Screen Capture
- Annotation: Skitch, Markup Hero, or built-in Preview
